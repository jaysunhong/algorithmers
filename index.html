Thanos will choose a random number at the start of the game.

There are six infinity stones below.

By clicking on an infinity stone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to Thanos' random number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above Thanos' random number.

The value of each infinity stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

Every time the game restarts, the values of the infinity stones will be reset.
